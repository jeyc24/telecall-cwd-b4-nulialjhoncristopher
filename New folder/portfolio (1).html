About Us

Welcome to J&J Petals & Threads Boutique! We are passionate about bringing the beauty of nature into your life through exquisite floral arrangements and handcrafted arts. Our shop is more than just a place to buy flowers; it's a creative hub where imagination blooms.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by craft artist Jessa and Jc, J&J Petals & Threads Boutique started as a small studio tucked away in a charming corner of the city. Emi's love for flowers and her knack for crafting beautiful arrangements quickly gained attention, and soon, the studio became a beloved destination for flower enthusiasts and fashion lovers alike.

Our Mission

At J&J Petals & Threads Boutique, our mission is to inspire and delight our customers with the finest quality flowers and artisanal crafts. We believe in the power of nature to uplift spirits and bring joy to everyday life. With each arrangement we create and every handmade craft we offer, we strive to spread happiness and positivity.

Our Services

Whether you're looking for a stunning bouquet for a special occasion, a unique gift for a loved one, or a hands-on crafting experience to unleash your creativity, J&J Petals & Threads Boutique has you covered. From custom floral arrangements to DIY workshops, we provide a range of services tailored to meet your needs.
